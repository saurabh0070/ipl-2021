Match 03



KKR's 100th ipl win!

IPL 2021's match no.3 is done and kkr is the team came out as a winner,and of course it's a 100th IPL win for the team.both team showed the winning intent but at last kkr grabbed it by bowling the last crucial overs very well.lets take a look at some of the highlights of match no.3:



Rana the rampage!


Nitish Rana started his ipl 2021 season with an excellent innings against srh.he came out to open the innings and looked good throughout the innings. he is enhancing really well at the opening position.in his blistering knock of 80(56) he added 9 fours and 4 sixes.let's see he continues the form throughout the season or not.



Tripathi's 50


After Gill departed very early captain Morgan sent tripathi at no.3 to show his game.he brilliantly grabbed the opportunity and showed his batting skills very well. along with Rana he continuesly accelerated the innings of kkr to make them to the smart total.young man scored 53 off just 28 balls.



Pandey's effort goes in vein

chasing the total of 188 srh lost their 2 wickets at early stage of saha(7) and warner(3).then Manish came out to bat and anchored the innings nicely and almost got his team close to the total.but in the final overs kkr bowled in control and pandey failed to win the match for team.scored a nice 61(44) hitting 2 fours and 3 sixes.



Others:


DK the finisher

Dinesh Karthik's major short cameo became the diffrence of the match.after losing important wickets DK came out and added the finishing touch to the innings with a beautiful 22 off just 9 balls.


Bairstow's position puzzle

SRH fans were expecting Bairstow to open the innings but he came to bat at no.4 position.although he was doing his job quite nicely,but opening the innings may has added the diffrence to the match as we all know how dangerous he is as an opener.let's see where the captian will use him for upcomin matches.
