Match 14

SRH's first win


SRH has finally opened their winning account against punjab kings with a huge 9 wickets win. after struggling in thier first 3 matches Warner should finally have some relaxing vibes around him. however punjab again struggled with their batting lineup which looks really destroying on paper.



Punjab's uneven batting collapse

Punjab is facing a batting lineup collapse. this is the second time they have made such a low score. punjab has some serious batting fire in their lineup but no one is managing to construct the innings after some early wickets. they just throw their wickets continuesly.



Khaleel Ahmed strikes

Khaleel played a major role in the batting collapse of punjab. he along with the others managed to stop punjab on low total which helped them for an easy win. khaleel picked 3 wickets in his spell. he needs to do that consistently to take srh at better position.



Bairstow's assault

Bairstow came out to open with Warner to chase down the total which was probably the easy one. and Bairstow just started his innings like typically he does always. a whole lot of aggression with a whole lot of class.he scored a good 63 runs and consolidated srh to win it.



Williamson brings stability

Finally SRH has added Williamson to their side and he provided his likes by simply anchoring the innings really well along with Bairstow. he brings a solid balance to the batting lineup of srh. he is the key player for them. let's see weather can he change the vibes for srh.
