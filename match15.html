Match 15

KKR surrendered after explosive fightback


It was raining sixes last night when CSK and KKR faced off each other. a pure thriller and a lethal fightback by the KKR which exposed the firepower of the lower order. they almost got the match in their hand but unluckily they failed and CSK got their another win.



power of Du Plesis

Faf proved that age is just a number for him. he scored an unbeaten 95 runs off just 60 balls. it was an absolute domination by Faf. he helped his team to put a gaint total against KKR.



Backing Gaikwad resulted rewardfull for CSK

After the failure in his initial innings Ruturaj Gaikwad excellently proved on the teams decesion on backing him. he pulled out a big start for CSK with Faf and scored his first 50 of the season.



Russel Muscle is back!

The monster Russel is backed his form against CSK by scoring a furious 50. he came out to bat after a major collision of the top order. it was 31 for 5 when he arrived and total to chase down was 221. but he showed his explosive ability and started thrashing CSK bowlers badly. unfortunatly leaving a ball costed him his imp wicket and a loss to his team.



Cummins exposed his batting power

After the wicket of Russel and Karthik it was almost lose of hope for KKR but few moments later it was all opposite. Cummins showed off his danger side in batting. it was an ultimate innings as he scored unbeaten 66 off just 34 balls. absolute Destruction.
