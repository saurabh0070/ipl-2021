Match 07

Magical MI


Mumbai Indians have been successful to defend another low score of the season of them. they are looking really balanced and positive in thier bowling. instead of the solid start of the SRH team, MI managed to defend the total of 150 against them. probably a "MI" thing.



Chahar the slayer again!

Rahul Chahar has fired again with the ball to grab the match for their team. Chahar is doing an excellent job since the last two matches. he managed to take the wickets at the crucial moments when it was required for the team. bowling excellently he grabbed 3 wickets and played the important role in the win.



Pandya's feilding contribution

Pandya failed to perform with the bat against the SRH bowling attack but still stealed the limelight through his streaky feilding. his bullet throws costed SRH the cruciak wickets of Warner and Samad. almost the turning point of the match.



The powerman Pollard

Pollard once again proved why he is called the most powerfull man of the cricket. coming with the form of 6 sixes in an over Pollard gave the important finishing to the team. he hitted the longest six of the season till now of 105 meters which was a pure rampage.



Bairstow's ruthless aggression

Bairstow was in a serious aggressive mood last night. he started the innings with an ultimate aggression and the thrashing the MI bowlers ruthlessly.but unluckily got hit wicket otherwise he was clinching the game towards SRH's favour.
