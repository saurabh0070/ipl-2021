Match 02




DC openers thrashed CSK single handedly

lot of action has been seen in the second match of ipl 2021 where DC and CSK were clashed with each other.it was a high scoring game on wankhede. CSK managed to make 188 in there first innings,which was an absolute challenge for the DC btting lineup.but the DC openers were outstanding clinching the match single handedly.let's take out a look on the key moments of the match:



The Prithvi "Show"


almost everyone was excited to see how prithvi is gonna perform because of his incredible performance in vijay hazare trophy. and he continued the form last night with his ruthless 72 runs off just 38 balls including 9 fours and 3 classy sixes. DC fans will expect that his form will be continued along the season.



Gabbar is back!


A vintage shikhar dhawan was seen in the last night's encounter.he played a very clean innings with some creative and excellent shots. he looked so good batting against CSK,although CSK is his favorite team to attack.just like last year gabbar thrashed each bowler of CSK. he managed to score 85 off 54 with 10 fours and 2 sixes.with this consistency this opening duo can be a success key for DC.



OLd is always Gold


Suresh Raina has made his comeback to the ipl with a classy raina 50.he introduced his arrival in style.he looked so good during his innings like he never stopped playing cricket.it was an intresting battle between ashwin and raina, but raina won it one sidedly. mr.ipl scored 54 off 36 including 3 fours and 4 sixes.although he was little unlucky to be run-out at crucial overs.



the imp english duo


The English duo of CSK looked good. both played important innings for the team. this duo can bring a great balance in the team in bowling as well as batting. both scored 30+ is their first match of season which is good signs for CSK.lets see what they can do best for the team ahead.
